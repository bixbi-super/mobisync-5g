What it does

Full protocol stack. Live. In Wireshark.

No export, no conversion, no waiting. Messages decode the moment they arrive over USB.

RRC

RRC + NAS Decode

Full OTA decode — MIB, SIB1/SIBs, RRC Setup, Security, UE Capability, Reconfig, Paging. NAS SM/MM messages including decrypted PDUs.

MAC

MAC / RLC Analytics

RACH Trigger and Msg1–Msg4 detail, PDSCH Stats, UL TB Stats, UL BSR, CSI Reports, RLC DL — slot-by-slot visibility into the scheduler.

L1

Physical Layer KPIs

Cell Search updates, RSRP/RSRQ/SNR for serving and neighbour cells, UL PHY channels — PUSCH, PUCCH, SRS — all live.

GUI

Clean PyQt Interface

Select exactly which log categories to enable — MAC, RRC, NAS, L1 — and hit Start. Wireshark opens and fills instantly.

LUA

Lua Extensibility

Plug in your own Wireshark Lua dissectors for vendor-specific or proprietary message formats. Full dissector and filter compatibility.

PCAP

Live + Offline Logging

Choose real-time Wireshark streaming or pcap dump for offline replay. Works on Ubuntu 22.04/24.04, Windows, and Raspberry Pi (offline).

Perfect for

Every phase of 5G development.

From initial gNB bring-up to field diagnostics, Mobisync fits naturally into your workflow.

01 — BRING-UP

Network Bring-Up

See instantly if the UE is stuck at MIB, SIB, RRC Setup, or Security stage. Detect attach and registration failures in real time.

02 — TUNING

RAN Exploration & Tuning

Validate changes to RAN or core parameters on the spot. Confirm UE capability negotiation, reconfig responses, and security setups.

03 — DEVELOPMENT

Feature Development & Testing

Debug handovers, cell selection/reselection, and advanced features. Correlate UE traces with gNB logs slot-by-slot.

04 — FIELD

Field Diagnostics

Instant insight into why a UE isn't attaching or hitting throughput targets. USB-based — fits right into your drive-test or demo kit.
